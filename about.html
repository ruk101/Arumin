Discover Our Process

At Arumin, we take pride in overseeing every step of the sapphire journey—from mine to market.

Step 1

Mining Rough Sapphires

Owning some of the finest sapphire mines across Sri Lanka, we begin by mining rough sapphires using a combination of human and mechanical methods, depending on the nature of the land. Throughout this process, every person involved receives a fair share of the proceeds from the mined sapphires, ensuring a commitment to ethical practices. On occasion, we also buy rough sapphires from vetted artisanal miners at fair prices, supporting and uplifting the gem mining community in Sri Lanka.

Step 2

Cutting and Polishing

Once the tough sapphires are extracted, our experts carefully sort and grade each stone. Some sapphires are immediately passed to our skilled craftsmen for cutting and polishing, while others remain in our inventory for bespoke cutting services, offered to select brands.

Step 3

Curating Collections

After the gemstones are cut and polished, they undergo a thorough selection process, ensuring that only the finest sapphires are presented to our partners. If the sapphires align with your specifications, they will be shared for your consideration.

There is no obligation to purchase any of the sapphires we present. We understand that your needs may change, and we will continue to offer new sapphires as they become available, ensuring you have access to the perfect stones when the time is right.

Our Commitment

Arumin is driven by a deep responsibility to uplift and support the communities that make our work possible.

At Arumin, we believe every Ceylon sapphire reflects the heart and spirit of Sri Lanka. Yet, despite being home to the world’s most sought-after gems, Sri Lanka often receives only a fraction of the value these sapphires fetch in international markets because many are not exported through official channels, or are resold abroad for higher prices, depriving the nation of much-needed foreign exchange. This imbalance affects not only our economy but also the communities that have nurtured this heritage for generations.

By choosing Arumin, you help ensure that the true value of these stones stays where it belongs, within Sri Lanka. We focus on keeping the economic benefits at home, supporting the people who are the backbone of this industry.

Your purchase does more than acquire a gemstone; it directly supports families and strengthens the local economy. Together, we can shift the story of Ceylon sapphires, ensuring that each stone sold shines with the promise of a better future for Sri Lanka.

Buy from Sri Lanka, For Sri Lanka. Because a truly priceless gem isn’t just one that is rare; it’s one that uplifts an entire nation.

At Arumin, we prioritize sustainability in all aspects of our operations. In most of the areas where we mine, traditional methods are used, which have a minimal impact on the environment. Once mining is complete, we take careful steps to rehabilitate the land, ensuring it is restored to its natural state. We also believe in fair compensation for our workers, providing fair wages and ensuring they receive a rightful share of the stones they mine.

Through our Sapphires for Smiles initiative, we allocate a portion of our profits to address the serious issue of child malnutrition in Sri Lanka. The World Food Program reports that nearly one-third of children under 5 in the country are malnourished, a challenge that has long-term impacts on their health and development.
